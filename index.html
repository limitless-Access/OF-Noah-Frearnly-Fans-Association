The Founding Charter

An institution for the seriously devoted.

"Enthusiasm, properly filed, becomes distinction."

The Noah Frearnly & Eligible Fans Association was chartered in 2015 on a simple, slightly absurd premise: that admiration, like membership in any respectable society, deserves paperwork. What began as a hand-kept list of a few dozen names has since grown into a fully indexed registry of one thousand fans, each holding a numbered Fan Code that is issued once, recorded permanently, and never reassigned.

We are not a fan club in the casual sense. We keep no group chat, run no merchandise table, and make no demands on anyone's time. We do exactly one thing, and we do it with excessive care: we verify who belongs, and we say so in writing. A Fan Code is not a discount or a perk — it is a matter of record, the Association's answer to the question "were you here first, and can you prove it?"

Membership was, and remains, by verification only. There is no application form to flatter, no fee to collect, and no tier of the Association that can be purchased outright. Standing is earned the old-fashioned way — through documented, dateable enthusiasm — and then entered into the ledger below, alphabetically, permanently, and without exception.

Grades of Standing

Three ways to be on the record.

Every member holds one of three grades, assigned at induction and reviewed each founding anniversary. Grade reflects tenure and documented conduct, not spend — the Association has never sold a rank, and does not intend to start.

Grade III
Correspondent
Entry standing · from induction

The default grade held by all new entries. A Correspondent is fully verified and fully listed — the Association draws no meaningful distinction in privilege below this line, only in seniority.

  • Full entry in the alphabetical Registry
  • Personal Fan Code, issued once
  • Instant verification lookup
Most Held
Grade II
Confidant
Awarded after 3 years of record

Reached automatically at the third founding anniversary of a member's induction, provided the file remains in good standing. No renewal, no notice required — the ledger simply updates itself.

  • Everything in Correspondent
  • Seniority flag visible on lookup
  • Listed among the earliest-verified names
Grade I
Laureate
Reserved · Founding Circle only

Held by the small number of names verified in the Association's first founding year, 2015. The grade cannot be applied for, transferred, or reissued — it exists only where the date already says so.

  • Everything in Confidant
  • Founding-year designation on file
  • Recorded in the Charter's original hand
Articles of Association

How the register is kept.

Adopted 2015 · Unamended
I.

On Enrollment

No member enrolls themselves. Entry to the Registry follows verification of standing by the Association's own process, at which point a Fan Code is generated and the member's name is filed under the appropriate letter.

II.

On the Fan Code

Each code is issued once, in the format NFA-####, and is permanently bound to the member it names. Codes are not transferable, resalable, or reassignable upon a member's withdrawal from public life.

III.

On Good Standing

A member remains in good standing indefinitely, by default. The Association reserves the right to annotate a file, but has, as a matter of policy, never once exercised it.

IV.

On Public Record

The Registry is published in full and searchable by name or code. The Association keeps no private tier of membership — what is true of one's standing is true in the ledger, for anyone to check.

V.

On Grade

Grade is calculated automatically from date of induction and requires no action from the member. The Association does not accelerate, backdate, or otherwise negotiate a member's grade.

VI.

On Purpose

This charter and registry are maintained for entertainment purposes only, in the spirit of the ledgers, societies, and chartered institutions it borrows its form from. No affiliation with Noah Frearnly is claimed or implied.

Questions of Standing

Frequently asked, formally answered.

How do I get a Fan Code?

Codes are issued through the Association's own verification process and cannot be requested, purchased, or self-assigned. If you believe you should be on file and are not, the Registry below remains the definitive record.

I've lost my Fan Code. Can it be reissued?

Codes are never reissued, but they are never lost, either — they live permanently in the ledger. Search the Registry by name to recover yours, then use the verification counter above to confirm it.

Does grade come with any privileges?

None beyond the record itself. Correspondent, Confidant, and Laureate are distinctions of tenure, not access — the Association has no gated content, no members-only anything. The ledger is the whole of the benefit, and we think that's rather the point.

Can membership be revoked?

In principle, under Article III. In the Association's ten years of record-keeping, it never has been.

Is this affiliated with Noah Frearnly?

No. The Association is an independent, unaffiliated registry, maintained for entertainment purposes only, in the style of the chartered societies and membership ledgers it borrows its form from.
